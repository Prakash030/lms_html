💬 How to handle class imbalance in ML models?
RM
Rahul Mehta1h ago
Try SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique) from imbalanced-learn. Also adjusting class weights in sklearn models using class_weight='balanced' is super effective and quick to implement.
KR
Dr. Kavitha R.45m ago
Both SMOTE and class weights are good. Also consider using precision-recall AUC instead of accuracy as your evaluation metric when dealing with imbalanced datasets. F1 score is also very informative here.
